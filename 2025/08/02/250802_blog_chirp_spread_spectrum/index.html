Paper 3 - Memoryless Continuous-Phase Chirp Spread Spectrum for Mud Pulse Telemetry

This paper considers the estimation for symbol duration and bandwidth under the underwater acoustic scenario. The receiver utilizes a concatenation of algorithms to analytically acquire the corresponding estimates, given as follows.

  • Memoryloss analog nonlinear preprocessor (MANP): suppress the impact of underwater noise (thus neglected).
  • Short-time Fractional Fourier Transform (ST-FRFT): Output an time-frequency image.
  • Edge detection: Convert the color image from TF image into a gray image.
  • Hough Transform: an effective image recognition method for a gray image.
  • Parameter Estimation: Calculate all bandwidth, duration, and chirp rate. Then k-means is employed to classify these parameter sets (by solely using bandwidth).
