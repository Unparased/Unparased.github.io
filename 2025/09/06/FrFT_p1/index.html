Digital Computation of the Fractional Fourier Transform (1996)

The FrFT has been widely used in a lot of fields, especially in communications. However, the operations with respect to the continuous domain can lead to considerable computational complexity. To mitigate this issue, several papers have considered the proposal of discrete FrFT (DFrFT). To start with, we simply provide the sampling-based form.

Sampling-based DFrFT

There are three primary papers delving into this topic.

  • Kraniauskas directly sampled the time and fractional Fourier domain.
  • Ozaktas derived the efficient and accurate DFrFT.(prevalent)
  • Pei defined another DFrFT form.(prevalent)
